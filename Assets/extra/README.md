# Bread Crumbs
## Summary
If you get hit by your enemies three times, its Game Over! 

## Buttons
> **Property**  | **Value**
> --------------|--------------------------------
> W             | Walks forward
> S             | Walks backward
> A             | Walks Left
> D             | Walks Right
